Features

We build tools that enhance data ownership and provide app interoperability!

Portal is your personal web front page for a pure browsing experience. You will enjoy a good choice of components including Quick Access, Widgets, and Workspace.

With Quick Access, you can left click to access your favorite web apps homepage and right click to access recently visited sites under the app.

With Widgets, you will login with OAuth and then be able to preview your apps before opening them--google calendar, gmail, notes, and more apps will be integrated soon.

With Workspace, you can put your tabs into different workspaces and manage them more conveniently.


What are our next steps?

  • Workspaces and frequently used web apps.
  • Calendar and notes.
  • Weather data and location.
  • Apps notifications (like Gmail).
  • More Widgets.
