Privacy Overview

Last updated Sep 7, 2020

Alora is committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your data. Our goal is to help you clean browsing history more efficiently and protect you from third-party trackers--all actions done locally.

We do not have a server nor do we have access to any of your information--everything is stored locally on your computer.

What information does Alora collect?

Alora collects zero information: we do not have a server.

Does Alora share or disclose my information?

No.
